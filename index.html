What I am working on

I am currently exploring a concept in computer vision where you want to find structures inside of an image. Unlike familar approaches in Modern Computer Vision focused on Segmentation and Classification via Machine Learning. My approach is more focused on attention and objective learning through the near infinite possible structures inside of a image.

I will not detail specifics. But to understand what I mean in my theory you can imagine the possible structures inside of a image being near infinite due to the number of possible explanations. Explanations are way of interpreting possible structures inside of image and using objectives we guide attention to find what explanation of a possible structure we are looking for.
